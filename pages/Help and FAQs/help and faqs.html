Help and FAQs

How do I park my car? ▼

To park your car, simply log in to the web app, check slot availability, select a parking spot, confirm your booking, and make a payment if required. The system provides real-time slot updates and Google Maps navigation to guide you to your reserved spot.

What are the parking rates? ▼

Parking rates vary based on location, duration, and vehicle. You can check the specific rates for your selected parking area within the app before booking. The system ensures transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

How can I pay for parking? ▼

You can pay for parking through credit/debit cards, online wallets (PayPal, Google Pay), and UPI. After a successful payment, you will receive a confirmation notification via SMS, email, or app notification, ensuring a seamless transaction experience.
